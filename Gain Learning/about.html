About Us

Who We Are

GAIN Learning Today Leading Tomorrow is a 100% woman-owned and majority black-owned company with a Level 1 B-BBEE status.

The organisation is fully accredited by W&R SETA, MICT SETA, Services SETA and the QCTO.

Since 2010, GAIN Learning has been at the forefront of transformative skills development and training, operating mainly in Gauteng and KwaZulu-Natal but not restricted to other provinces with flexible, mobile training teams.

Our Identity and Purpose

Our name encapsulates our purpose: by gaining knowledge today, learners are empowered to lead tomorrow. We champion transformation, focusing on building confidence, character, and competence through education.

"You were born with the seed of greatness in you." - Myles Munroe

GAIN Learning's quest for GAINS—Growth, Achievement, Innovation, Nurturing, and Sustainability—is embedded in every service we deliver. We serve as a bridge to better opportunities for those historically marginalized and underserved.

Our Vision

We aim to provide cost-effective, high-quality training that empowers both individuals and corporate teams to grow. Our programs enhance personal development, workforce productivity, and help organisations meet strategic goals through practical, sustainable skills development.

Our Mission

To deliver outstanding training through a dedicated team of experienced professionals. We uplift communities and businesses alike through targeted learning that promotes decent work, economic growth, and a lifelong learning culture.
